Our Network in Action

Our volunteers in the field, assisting patients on the home front.

These days, as the entire country holds its breath against Iran and the frontlines are burning, another frontline operates quietly but critically right under the radar.

"Imagine an elderly person or a chronic patient, sitting in a bomb shelter, anxious about the security situation - and then discovering their life-saving medication is running out."

We urgently brought in additional pharmacists, delivery vehicles, and motorcycles that race even under rocket sirens.
